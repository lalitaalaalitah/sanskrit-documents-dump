Sanskrit Mailing list

An unmoderated mailing list exists for people interested in discussing about Sanskrit literature, grammar, subhashitas, scriptures, etc. Everybody from beginners to experts is welcome to join the list.

If you are interested in joining the group, goto the "subscribe" section of http://mailman.cs.utah.edu/listinfo/sanskrit/ and follow instructions.

Also join the Sanskrit sabhA (forum) at http://sanskrit.bhaarat.com
