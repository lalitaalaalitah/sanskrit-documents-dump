SANSKRIT BHARATI PUBLICATIONS
					  
Sanskrit Bharati has published more than 40 books and audio cassettes
to teach simple Sanskrit. These books are based on the vast experience
of the organization in teaching spoken Sanskrit to millions of people
all over the world.
