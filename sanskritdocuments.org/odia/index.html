॥ ନମୋ ନମଃ ॥

ଭାଷାସୁ ମୁଖ୍ୟା ମଧୁରା ଦିଵ୍ୟା ଗୀର୍ଵାଣ ଭାରତୀ ।
ତସ୍ୟାଂ ହି କାଵ୍ୟଂ ମଧୁରଂ ତସ୍ମାଦପି ସୁଭାଷିତମ୍ ॥

Among the divinely sweet  spoken words, 
Sanskrit, Bharata's descendants spell,
Poetry is sweeter still; yet, 'subhashita', 
The eloquent counsel, surpasses them all.

Sanskrit Documents in Odia Script

Welcome to the collection and links for Sanskrit documents in Odia script.

    This is an effort to publish the religious stotras, vedic sukta, and books in Odia (Oriya) script. The files are presented in PDF format using "dhwani-Unicode" software. Some of the documents are displayed with the vedic accent (swara marks) for easy chanting.


    Volunteers are needed to expand this collection for the benefit of all Odia speaking friends. Please send your suggestions and comments to odiaorg@gmail.com or visit odia.org for additional details.
