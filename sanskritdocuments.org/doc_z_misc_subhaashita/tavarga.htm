tR^iNa.n khaaditi kedaare jala.n pibati palvale |
dugdha.n yachchhati lokebhyo dhenurno jananii priyaa ||1||

tadevaasya.n para.n mitra.n yatra saN^kramati dvaya.n |
dR^ishhTe sukha.n cha duHkha.n cha pratichchhaayeva darpaNe ||2||

tyaaga eka guNaH shlaaghyaH kimanyairguNaraashhibhiH |
tyaagaajjagati puujyante pashupaashhaaNapaadapaaH ||3||

taatena kathita.n putra patra.n likha mamaaGYayaa |
natena likhita.n patra.n pituraaGYaa ma laN^ghitaa ||4||

te yaanti tiirtheshhu budhaaH ye shambhoduu.rravartinaH |
yasya gauriishvarashchitte tiirtha.n bhoja para.n hi saH ||5||

tyajanti mitraaNi dhanairvihiina.n putraashcha daaraashcha suhR^ijjanaashcha |
tamarthavanta.n punaraashrayanti artho hi loke manushhasya bandhuH ||6||

taaDitaaH piiDitaa ye syustaanmametyabhyudiirayet.h |
sa saadhuravagantavyastatra drashhTavya iishvaraH ||7||

tR^iNaadapi laghustuula.n tuulaadapi cha yaachakaH |
vaayunaa ki.n na niito.asau maamaya.n praarthayediti ||8||

tushhTo.api raajaa yadi sevakebhyaH bhaagyaatpara.n naiva dadaati kiJNchit.h |
aharnisha.n varshhati vaarivaahaastathaapi patratritayaH palaashaH ||9||

tilavatsnigdha.n mano.astu vaaNyaa.n guDavanmaadhuryam.h 
tilaguDalaDDukavat.h sambandhe astu suvR^ittatvam.h |
astu vichaare shubhasaN^kramaNa.n maN^galaaya yashase 
kalyaaNI saN^kraantirastu vaH sadaahamaasha.nse ||10||
