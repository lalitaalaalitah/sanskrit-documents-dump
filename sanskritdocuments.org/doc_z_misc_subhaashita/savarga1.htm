sadaachaareNa sarveshhaa.n shuddha.n bhavati maanasam.h |
nirmala.n cha vishuddha.n cha maanasa.n devamandiram.h ||1||

subhaashhitarasaasvaadaH sajjanaiH saha saN^gatiH |
sevaa vivekibhuupasya duHkhanirmuulana.n trayam.h ||2||

sadaya.n hR^idaya.n yasya bhaashhita.n satyabhuushhitam.h |
dehaH parahite yasya kalistasya karoti kim.h ||3||

sukhasyaanantara.n duHkham.h duHkhasyaanantara.n sukham.h |
na nitya.n labhate duHkha.n na nitya.n labhate sukham.h ||4||

sthaanabhrashhTA na shobhante dantaaH keshA nakhA naraaH |
iti viGYaaya matimaan.h svasthaana.n na parityajet.h ||5||

sukulajanma vibhuutiranekadhaa priyasamaagamasaukhyaparamparaaH |
budhajane gurutaa vimala.n yashaH bhavati puNyataroH phalamiidR^isham.h ||6||

sundaro.api sushiilo.api kuliino.api mahaadhanaH |
na shobhate vinaa vidyaa.n vidyaa sarvasya bhuushhaNam.h ||7||

sarve yatra vinetaaraH sarve paNDitamaaninaH |
sarve mahattvamichchhanti raashhTra.n tadavasiidati ||8||

svabhaavo nopadeshena shakyate kartumanyathaa |
sutaptamapi paaniiya.n punargachchhati shiitataam.h ||9||

samudravasane devi parvataavalibhuushhite |
vishhNupatni namastubhya.n paadasparsha.n kshamasva me ||10||


sarvathaa santyajedvaada.n na kaJNchinmarmaNi spR^ishet.h |
sarvaan.h parityajedarthaan.h svaadhyaayasya virodhinaH ||11||

suurya.n bhartaaramutsR^ijya parvata.n maaruta.n girim.h |
svajaati.n muushhikaa praaptaa svabhaavo duratikramaH ||12||

sa jaato yena jaatena yaati va.nshaH samunnatim.h |
parivartini sa.nsaare mR^itaH ko vaa na jaayate ||13||

sampuurNakumbho na karoti shabdam.h ardho ghaTo ghoshhamupaiti nuunam.h |
vidvaan.h kuliino na karoti garva.n muDhaastu jalpanti guNairviihiinaaH ||14||

sauvarNaani sarojaani nirmaatu.n santi shilpinaH |
tatra saurabhanirmANe chaturashchaturaananaH ||15||

sampado mahataameva mahataameva chaapadaH |
vardhate kshiiyate chandro na tu taaraagaNaH kvachit.h ||16||

satyaanusaariNii lakshmiiH kiirtistyaagaanusaariNii |
abhyaasasaariNii vidyaa buddhiH karmaanusaariNI ||17||

sevitavyo mahaavR^ikshaH phalachchhaayaasamaanvitaH |
yadi daivaat.h phala.n naasti chhaayaa kena nivaaryate ||18||

sarvatra deshe guNavaan.h shobhate prerito naraH |
maNiH shiirshe gale baahau yatra kutraapi shobhate ||19||

svaya.n paJNchamukhaH putrau gajaananashhaDaananau |
digambaraH katha.n jiivet.h annapuurNaa na chedgR^ihe ||20||
