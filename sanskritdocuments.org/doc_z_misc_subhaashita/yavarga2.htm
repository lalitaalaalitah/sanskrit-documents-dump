yatra rAmakathAgAna.n tatrAste hanumAn.h yathA |
sa.nskR^itadhyayanaM yatra tatra sa.nskR^itidarshanam.h ||21||

yadevopanata.n duHkhAt.h sukha.n tadrasavattaram.h |
nirvANAya taruchchhAyA taptasya hi visheshhataH ||22||

yaH samutpatita.n krodha.n kshamayaiva nirasyati |
yathoragastvacha.n jIrNA.n sa vai purushha uchyate ||23||

yogakshemAya rAshhTrasya sabhyatAyAshcha sa.nskR^iteH |
naivAnyo vidyate panthA lokasa.nghaTana.n vinA ||24||

yA.n chintayAmi satata.n mayi sA viraktA 
sApyanyamichchhati jana.n sa jano.anyasaktaH |
asmatkR^ite cha paritushhyati kAchidanyA 
dhik.h tA.n cha ta.n cha madana.n cha imA.n cha mA.n cha ||25||

yayoraiva sama.n vitta.n yayoraiva sama.n kulam.h |
tayomai.rtrirvivAhashcha na tu pushhTavipushhTayoH ||26||

yad.h yad.h vibhUtimatsattva.n shrImadUrjitameva vA |
tattadevAvagachchha tva.n mama tejo.a.nshasambhavam.h ||27||

yaH pUtanAmAraNalabdhakIrtiH kAkodaro yena vinItadarpaH |
yashodayAla.nkR^itamUrtiravyAt.h patiryadUnAmathavA raghUNAm.h ||28||

yadi vA yAti govindo mathurAtaH punaH sakhI |
rAdhAyA nayanadvandve rAdhAnAmaviparyayaH ||29||


yatra nAryastu pUjyante ramante tatra devatAH |
yatra tAstu na pUjyante sarvAstatrAphalAH kriyAH ||30||

yatra nAsti dadhimanthanaghoshhaH tatra no laghulaghuni shishuni |
yatra nAsti gurugauravapUjA tAni ki.n bata gR^ihANi vanAni ||31||
