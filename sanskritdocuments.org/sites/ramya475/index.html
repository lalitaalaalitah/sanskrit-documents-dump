        Quote from Shri P. R. Ramachander:
        "I am working for next generation who would have forgotten Sanskrit as well as their mother toungue. I have a feeling that what I do would
        be understood by them and tell them about the majesty of our Hindu religious thought. My transliterations are only a guide to recognition
        and understanding and definitely not for people to read, may be my thought process is different. But large number of people
        are preserving our rich heritage for the people who will understand Sanskrit or their mother toungue.
        I never claim nor do I think that I am doing anything great but I do think that I am doing this as an instrument of God.
        May God bless you all, With best wishes, Ramachander"
