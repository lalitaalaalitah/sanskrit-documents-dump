      Mann Ki Baat in Sanskrit

        Mann Ki Baat is a monthly Hindi broadcast by Prime Minister Narendra Modi addressing citizens of India. Its Sanskrit translation and audio are prepared by Dr. Baldevanand Sagar. The available texts and audio clips are linked from the page manogatam (mann-ki-baat) Sanskrit.
