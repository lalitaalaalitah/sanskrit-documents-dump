Jivanmuktanandalahari

 

This is a short work of 17 slokas attributed to Sri Sankaracharya. It is an interesting description of how a Jivanmukta conducts himself in the world.

The slokas in Devanagari script, with English transliteration and translation can be seen at the following website:

 

http://www.kamakoti.org/shlokas/kshlok23.htm
