In these web pages, the verses of vAlmIki rAmAyaNa, so far encoded for Sanskrit 98 and Translit 98 fonts are experimentally transposed to UTF-8 encoding. Readers are requested to tune their machines to enable International Support to show Indic languages, Sanskrit 2003 and URW Palladio ITU fonts.

Included here is a 3 MB pdf file of Introduction, which contains a detailed preface and exhaustive notes given by Shreemaan C. R. Srinivasa Ayyangar in his prose translation iof Valmiki Ramayana, a 1910 publication, of which only bAla and ayodhya kANDA-s are available in the corpus of Digital Library of India.
