Links

The Association for Yoga Studies - Uk wide yoga association open to all those interested in the “viniyoga” tradition stemming from Desikachar and his father Krishnamacharya - all its teachers are trained in this tradition : www.ays.org.uk
aYs Scotland - the Scottish subsidiary of aYs: www.ays-scotland.org.uk
Yoga Scotland - governing body for Yoga in Scotland: www.yogascotland.org.uk
Krishsnamacharya Yoga Mandiram - the home base of the “viniyoga” approach to yoga : www.kym.org
Yogaweb - offers a comprehensive guide to the ancient art and science of yoga : http://www.yogaweb.com
Sadhana Mala - the website of Ranju Roy and Dave Charlton, who offer all sorts of courses in the “viniyoga” approach: www.sadhanamala.com
If you are looking for a yoga class or individual lessons in Edinburgh, have a look at Lynne Scott’s website: http://www.yogaforyou-edinburgh.co.uk
