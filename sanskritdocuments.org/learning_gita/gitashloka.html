bhagavad giitaa
    verse-a-day-utility

    • Display a shloka on a daily basis
    • Display a randomly chosen sloka
    • Display the next sloka in the database
    • Run from your autoexec.bat or .login file
    • Invoke it every time you take a break !

    Options are available to choose any combinations of the sloka / translation / word meanings. The Slokas can be viewed in Devanagari font using one of -itxm/-itxweb/-itxpc options. See the readme.txt for further details.

    Utility Contents:

    • Dr. Ramanand Sagar's English translation
    • Sri Prabhupada's word meanings
    • Sri Madhvacharya's Gita Bhashya & Taatparya English translation.
