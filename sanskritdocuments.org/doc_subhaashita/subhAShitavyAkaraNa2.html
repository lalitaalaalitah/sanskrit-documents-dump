A bald person on (his) head being scorched by the rays of the Lord of the day (Sun) , wishing to go to a shady place, by chance, sat below a Palmyra (Tad) tree. A large fruit dropped from the top of the tree and broke his head with a loud sound. (Thus ), wherever an unfortunate person goes, there (he) recieves misfortunes..
