Sanskrit Documents in audio format

    Following is a list of sites where one can listen to stotras and bhajans mostly in real audio format. The stotras and artists, at some locations, are listed for reference. Most of the texts are available among Sanskrit Documents Collection.

    There are a number of video clips related to personality of Satya Sai Baba and projects undertaken by him at http://www.saibabafilms.com/. There is a video rendering in the end of Hymns from the Vedas with Vedic chanting and associated Sanskrit text. The text was generated using Itranslator98 software. The chanting is by Pundit Narendra Kapre

    If you find new stotras in audio/text format please write to sanskrit@cheerful.com.
