Ramayana - Discourses at Bahrain Satsangh

Vasanta Navaratri and Rama Navami was celebrated at our Satsangh in Bahrain recently. On this occasion, the satsanghii-s delivered short discourses on all the seven kaaNDa-s of the Ramayana. Many of the speakers were attempting this exercise for the very first time. It hence involved a lot of preparation and effort. These discourses will be useful for giving a brief insight into what the Ramayana is all about.

Ramayana talks given at the satsangh during 1999, 1998 and 1997 Sundarkand talk are linked on the left. Heartfelt thanks to Sarojaji for compiling and formatting the talks for web site.

Inspiring Articles from Divine Life Society's web site

Pannir has uploaded two articles by Holy Master Gurudev Sri Swami Sivanandaji Maharaj during Holy Ramnavami on March 25th 1999. The first is an article on the Ramayana. In this article, Gurudev explains the origins of this sacred scripture and also a synopsis of Valmiki's Ramayana. This article is at:

http://www.rsl.ukans.edu/~pkanagar/divine/religions/ramayana.htm

The second article is a biography of Samartha Ramdas who is a great devotee of Lord Rama and the Guru of Shivaji, the founder of the kingdom of Maharashtra. This article is at:

http://www.rsl.ukans.edu/~pkanagar/divine/saints/samartha_ramdas.htm

Two inspiring articles on Ramanavami are also available at the Divine Life Society's web site. One is by Holy Master Sri Swami Sivanandaji Maharaj and the other is by Sri Swami Krishnanandaji Maharaj. Both these articles will be of immense help in chiselling our character to that of Lord Rama's. The articles are at:

http://www.rsl.ukans.edu/~pkanagar/divine/religions/ramanavami.htm

http://www.rsl.ukans.edu/~pkanagar/divine/religions/ramanavami_krishna.htm
